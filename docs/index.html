Stake Shield

StakeShield is a blockchain audit company that focuses on smart-contract audits, on chain analysis and crypto custody. We are committed to providing our clients with best practices and industry leading security safeguards in the crypto space. Our goal is to help transform your ICO or digital assets investment into a more secure and professional experience by identifying potential vulnerabilities at the earliest stages of development. We will provide a comprehensive audit that addresses the most common issues found in blockchain projects and also serve as a guide for organizations seeking to adopt this technology.

What We Do

Smart Contract Audit

We audit smart contract to ensure that it is secure, functions properly, and follows industry best practices. This can involve reviewing the code of a smart contract to ensure that it is free of bugs and vulnerabilities, testing the system to see how it performs under different scenarios, and checking that the system adheres to relevant standards and regulations.

On chain analysis

We analyzinge transactions and other data that is recorded on the blockchain to gain insights into the behavior of the users of the blockchain, the usage of the blockchain, or other aspects of the blockchain. On-chain analysis can be useful for a variety of purposes, such as identifying trends, detecting anomalies and potential money laundering. It can also be used to improve the security and performance of a blockchain.

Crypto custody and risk assessment

The goal of crypto custody is to protect against theft, loss, or unauthorized access to the cryptocurrencies in question. This is an important consideration for both individual investors and institutions that hold large amounts of cryptocurrency.
